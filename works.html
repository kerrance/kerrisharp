Promotions.

Throughout the Summer of 2020, I spent most of my working hours at Opia building the LG Home Appliance platform.

The website allows LG to incentivise the sale of white goods to their customers across the USA. The current and future promotions we will run on the platform will encourage customers to "bundle" together different types of appliances to apply for greater rewards (primarily, cashback). This project consumed many hours and lots of overtime to complete across the summer.

The LG Home Appliance platform is an SPA made with VueJS. Working on this project was my first experience with automated testing for the frontend: particularly using Cypress and ESLint. My main area of focus was setting up the frontend features to match the client's expectations. I also used our internal APIs to set up the required data and logic for reward calculations.

Now that it has launched, my work throughout Autumn 2020 will focus on working with my team to expand the website across several promotions in the lead up to Black Friday and Christmas 2020. The platform will continue to run for the next 2 years with many promotions still to come.

Although tempting to list the other projects I have worked on at Opia, they often go live and then end within a few months. This fast turnaround makes cataloguing my work very difficult! If you're interested in seeing more examples of my recent promotional work, please contact me directly.
