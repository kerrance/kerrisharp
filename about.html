About.

Hello, my name is Kerri.

Who?

I'm a British web developer currently living in Somerset, England.

I've been making websites on-and-off for over 20 years. What started as a hobby became my main gig a few years ago.

Before going full-time as a web developer, I worked in the music industry as a record label manager. I've also worked as a games journalist, radio presenter, club DJ and even one time as a cocktail barman.

When not programming, I enjoy public speaking, writing, podcasting, DJing, making cocktails, video editing, tennis and collecting music.

Why?

I always want to create honest, well-designed bespoke websites and emails from scratch. I use a variety of tools, frameworks and languages to achieve this goal.

I like to keep my email inbox at zero. I review all of my projects weekly to ensure my next steps are clear. I hit my deadlines by maintaining a thorough to-do list and promptly addressing any blockers.

When I can, I'll work on something special for a friend, family member or colleague. Most of my side-projects are open source and publicly available on GitHub.
